About me

My name is Renzo Vargas Torres, I was born in Lima, Peru. I moved to Israel in 2008 with my parents when I was 15.
In 2016 I started my studies in Sound Engineering at Sapir College. Since 2018 I've worked as a Sound Recordist for the TV and Film industry.
I got to sound through music, since I was a child I loved to play the guitar.
My first encounter with programming was in January this year and I got fascinated by the mix of creativity and logical thinking this field requires. So I decided to give it a chance.
